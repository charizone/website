Initial research

Use our search platform to easily compare, analyse and navigate over 177,000 UK charities. Access 100+ data points pulled from the most reliable sources used in the sector.

Uncover new charities and causes while waving goodbye to manual spreadsheets and Google searches.

Comprehensive reports

Enhance your decision-making with data visualisation tools, offering centralised, structured and comparable data points from the most reliable sources in the sector.

Then use comprehensive Charity Performance Reports to add valuable insight, using our expert-led, in-depth and transparent analysis based on sector codes and conducts of best practice.

Ongoing support

Enjoy auto-updating data across the whole system, making annual reviews a breeze.

Get alerted when a charity you follow has updated its accounts, appeared in the news, or made a change to its board.

Let Charizone take care of the legwork, while you spend time building long-lasting and sustainable relationships with your charities of choice.
