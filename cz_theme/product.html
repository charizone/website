Charizone

UK charity research, reworked

Traditionally, charitable research has been time-consuming, difficult and inefficient.

Not anymore.

Quick

This is the first time such comprehensive UK charity data has been available to search, filter and analyse from one place.

Reliable

We only pool data from official and regulated sources, so you can trust the information you see is correct and up-to-date.

Efficient

Say no to outdated Excel spreadsheets, long-winded Google searches and settling for ‘good enough’. This is your research workflow, redefined.

The journey

We’re here to help you throughout your giving journey, from initial research, to due diligence, to ongoing support and relationship building.

  • Search
  • Shortlist
  • Analyse

Search

Use the dynamic smart search bar and pre-set filtering functionalities to quickly create a refined list of charities that align with your specific criteria.

Shortlist

Enhance your decision-making with data visualisation tools, offering centralised, structured and comparable data points from 12 reliable sources.

Analyse

Use comprehensive Performance Reports to add valuable insight, using our expert-led, in-depth and transparent analysis based on sector codes and conducts of best practice.

The features

Everything you need to do your research well. We’re small and agile; which means we’re constantly adding new tools, and building new features based on our users’ requests.
