Hi, we’re Charizone

We help organisations and individuals to scale their impact, and give with even more confidence.

By making charitable research better, easier, quicker and more accessible.

Our journey so far

From our humble beginnings in 2020, to all the users benefitting from our platform today.

The problem

We understand the challenges you face—fragmented, unstructured charity data and no clear standards to guide decision-making, with over 177,000 charities to choose from and manual processes slowing you down.

The result is a time-consuming, insular, and difficult-to-scale approach that lacks clarity and confidence.

The solution

Our innovative platform, Atlas, helps navigate the vast number of charities registered in the UK, aggregates extensive data from various sources into a user-friendly dashboard and offers deeper analysis using our Charizone Charity Performance Framework which defines what a good charity looks like based on existing sector codes and standards.

This allows users to source and check new charities, save time and stay up to date with the charities they care about.

Trusts and Foundations

Funders regularly search for, screen, and support charities that align with their values and priorities, all while fostering long-term relationships and keeping open dialogue.

Our platform streamlines due diligence, identifies high-impact opportunities, and keeps trusts and foundations updated with notifications. A portfolio view allows them to track and monitor their impact.

This enhances transparency, improves efficiency, and reduces manual work, supporting more effective, data-driven grantmaking, while freeing time to build relationships with charities.

Philanthropic advisors

Philanthropic advisors aim to expand their knowledge of charities and conduct focused searches for programmes that align with specific causes

Charizone makes this process easier by offering precise, cause-based search tools. Advisors can quickly explore and compare opportunities to find the best fit for their donors’ priorities and existing portfolios.

This helps advisors make more targeted recommendations, saves time on research, and allows them to offer more personalised, impactful advice.

Charities

Charities benefit from our Charity Performance Framework report, which provides an independent overview of their performance based on sector codes and guidelines.

The report offers a transparent and detailed overview of a charity’s impact, governance, and finances, designed to support continuous improvement and strategic growth.

These reports improve communication with philanthropists and funders through clear, consistent insights. They also support internal dialogues, helping leadership teams and boards stay aligned.

Sarah G

Founder & CEO

  • What’s your Charizone story?

    My Charizone story comes from a genuine passion for the charity sector, my love of data, and a relentless, solution-focused mindset.

    After years of supporting causes that I cared about, I kept running into the same issue: there was simply no clear, accessible information to help me make confident decisions. It was frustrating. I wanted to get a better sense of the mission, vision, and work behind the charities I was supporting, but instead, I found myself relying too heavily on brand and marketing. It was difficult to find tools or resources that could guide me through the process.

    With my background in numbers and data, I began to wonder if there was a way to bring data-driven insights into the charity world in a simpler, more effective way. I realised that by pooling together data and defining clear standards, we could begin to tell stories about charities in a more consistent, transparent way.

    That’s when Charizone was born. Teaming up with David Kane, a top expert in charity data, we set out to build something that could change the game. It wasn’t easy—there were plenty of challenges along the way—but the idea kept driving us forward. Now, Charizone is growing, connecting charities and philanthropists, and making the process of giving much more straightforward. And we’re just getting started!

  • Where do I know you from?

    My journey to get here has been shaped by a mix of experiences that have all led me to this point.

    I studied Business and Economics at the University of Edinburgh after spending my school years in Jordan, and from there, I built a twelve-year career in banking, developing a strong business and financial foundation. Over time, though, I found myself drawn toward work that could drive meaningful change.

    That pull led me into Impact Finance, taking on roles in FinTech and later ESG within the private sector. Alongside this, I’ve been fortunate to work with both local and international charities, ensuring purpose has always remained central to what I do. Today, I’m proud to serve as Treasurer on the Board of the UK Sustainable Investment & Finance Association, staying closely involved with efforts to shape a more sustainable future.

    These days, I live in London, where I enjoy spending time with my family and escaping to the outdoors whenever I can. And when I’m not working? You’ll likely find me on a Pilates mat or enjoying a good coffee somewhere in the city.

  • What are you excited about?

    We’re set to transform the charity sector. Charizone is creating tools that prioritise trust, transparency, and real impact, making giving smarter, simpler and more rewarding than ever.

    I’m inspired by how we can scale the incredible work of philanthropists, helping them support causes that align with their values while building deeper engagement with donors. Charizone makes generosity more informed and intentional.

    It’s fantastic to see how we’re helping charities showcase their true value, building a stronger, more credible sector. We’re not just helping charities attract more support for their mission—we’re empowering them to thrive.

    At its core, Charizone harnesses the power of data and technology for good, ensuring every pound given is more impactful.
